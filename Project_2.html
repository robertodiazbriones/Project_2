Segregation in Public Schools

We have data on racial demographics and resources of different schools and school districts. We scraped data from the web on racial demographics and locations of Michigan Schools. We, also, have a dashboard with geoplotting that shows racial composition of different schools and school districts. Our plot resource metrics vs racial composition targets on a selection of schools/districts. We will begin presenting javascript, leaflet and plotly datasets.
